Community Guidelines

Welcome to the SkunkStream community! To ensure a positive experience for all members, we ask that you adhere to the following guidelines:

1. Be Respectful

Treat all community members with respect. Discrimination, harassment, or hate speech of any kind will not be tolerated.

2. No Spamming or Self-Promotion

Please refrain from posting spam, advertisements, or excessive self-promotion. If you want to share something, ensure it is relevant and adds value to the conversation.

3. Keep Content Appropriate

All content shared within the community should be suitable for all ages. Inappropriate content, including graphic violence, adult material, or offensive language, is prohibited.

4. Stay On-Topic

Conversations should remain relevant to the community's focus. Off-topic discussions can be confusing for others and will be moderated as necessary.

5. Protect Privacy

Do not share personal information about yourself or others. Respect the privacy of all community members. If you are sharing a story or experience, avoid using identifiable information.

6. Report Issues

If you see something that violates these guidelines, please report it to the moderators immediately. We are here to help maintain a safe and enjoyable community.

7. Be Constructive

When providing feedback or criticism, do so constructively. Focus on the issue, not the individual, and aim for a positive outcome.

8. No Impersonation

Do not impersonate other users, moderators, or public figures. This can create confusion and erode trust within the community.

9. Intellectual Property

Respect the intellectual property rights of others. Do not share content that you do not own or have permission to use. Always provide credit where it is due.

10. Avoid Disruptive Behavior

Disruptive behavior, including excessive arguing, trolling, or inciting conflict, is not allowed. Keep discussions civil and focused.

11. Participate Actively

Engage with other community members by participating in discussions, asking questions, and sharing your own insights. A vibrant community is built on active participation!

12. Follow Additional Rules

Be aware of any additional rules or guidelines that may be specific to certain areas of the community. These may vary based on context and should always be followed.

13. Have Fun!

We encourage you to participate actively, share your thoughts, and enjoy the community experience!

Thank you for being a part of SkunkStream! Let's make this community a great place for everyone.
