Account Verification Information

Account Verified: ✔ Aqua Checkmark

To obtain an Account Verified status, follow these steps:

  • 1. Follow the Rules and Guidelines: Ensure your content adheres to the platform's community standards.
  • 2. Reach a minimum of 10K followers or subscriptions: Building your audience is essential for verification.
  • 3. Ensure your account age is at least 3 months: Verification is typically reserved for established accounts.
  • 4. Provide Authentic Content: Consistently post original and engaging content relevant to your audience.

Once the above criteria are met, your account will be reviewed to confirm eligibility for verification.

Note: Verification is not guaranteed even if all criteria are met. The review process considers various factors including account activity and engagement.
